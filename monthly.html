Fantasy

Six of Crows

by Leigh Bardugo

Leigh Bardugo’s Six of Crows is a fantasy novel set in the city of Ketterdam, inspired by Dutch Republic–era Amsterdam. The novel is the first in a duology and is part of the Grishaverse. The novel follows a crew of six young adults as they attempt to liberate a scientist from the Fjerdan government, getting rich in the process. It is told from the third-person viewpoints of seven characters, five of whom are members of the heist crew.

Red Queen

by Victoria Aveyard

Red Queen is a story about Mare Barrow, a 17 year old girl who lives in a world where status depends on the colour of your blood. People with red blood are commoners while those with silver are royals who possess special powers. Mare is a Red living with her younger sister, mother and father whilst her brothers are off fighting in the war. With her best friend Kilorn, she is taken away to serve the King. Trapped with the Silvers hiding secrets, Mare must use her new position and person to bring down the regime from the inside.

Legend

by Marie Lu

From different worlds, June and Day have no reason to cross paths—until the day June's brother is murdered and Day becomes the prime suspect. Day is in a race for his family's survival, while June wants to avenge her brother's death. But in a turn of events, the two uncover the truth of what has really brought them together, and the sinister lengths their country will go to keep its secrets.
